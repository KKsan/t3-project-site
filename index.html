Discovery Tool

The discovery tool is currently in alpha. It is being worked on as part of CS577a at USC, in collaboration with System1 over the Fall 2018 semester. The preliminary tool has been envisioned as a web-based system that allows advertisers and content creators to identify trending topics and products.

Our data and trend reporting can be used by clients to spark ideas for original content and product expansion. It can also be used at System 1 internally.
